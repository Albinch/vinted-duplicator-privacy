Privacy Policy - Vinted Duplicator

Last updated: November 17, 2024

Overview

Vinted Duplicator is a Chrome extension that helps you save time when creating Vinted listings. We take your privacy seriously and do not collect any personal data.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal information.

This extension does not:

  • Track your browsing history
  • Collect personal information
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Access your Vinted account credentials

Data Storage

The only data stored is your saved templates, which are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.local API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible by us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the extension

Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions:

storage

Used to save your templates locally in your browser.

activeTab

Used to read listing information from Vinted pages and fill forms when you apply a template.

Host permissions (vinted.fr, vinted.com, etc.)

Required to interact with Vinted pages and automate form filling.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
